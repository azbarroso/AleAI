## Workspace Plans

### Hobby
- **Price:** $0/month
- **Users:** Single user
- **Projects:** 1 project, 2 environments
- **Bandwidth:** 100 GB/month
- **Pipeline Minutes:** 500/month
- **Features:** Full-stack deployment, managed datastores, custom domains (up to 2), global CDN, basic security, email support

### Professional
- **Price:** $19/user/month
- **Users:** Up to 10
- **Projects:** Unlimited
- **Bandwidth:** 500 GB/month
- **Pipeline Minutes:** 500/user/month
- **Additional:** Horizontal autoscaling, preview environments, private links (up to 3), chat support, HTTP request logs

### Organization
- **Price:** $29/user/month
- **Users:** Unlimited
- **Projects:** Unlimited
- **Bandwidth:** 1 TB/month
- **Pipeline Minutes:** 500/user/month
- **Additional:** 100 webhooks, audit logs, SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA compliance, 30-day log retention

### Enterprise
- **Price:** Custom
- **Support:** Dedicated support + success engineer, private Slack, migration support, architecture review, SLAs, guaranteed uptime
- **Security:** SAML SSO, SCIM provisioning
