## MongoDB Atlas (Cloud)

### Free Tier (M0)
- **Price:** $0/hour
- **Storage:** 512 MB
- **RAM/vCPU:** Shared
- **Limits:** 100 ops/second, 32MB sort memory

### Flex Cluster
- **Price:** $0.011/hour (up to $30/month)
- **Storage:** 5 GB
- **RAM/vCPU:** Shared
- **Pricing by ops/sec:** 0-100: $8/month; 100-200: $15/month; 200-300: $21/month; 300-400: $26/month; 400-500: $30/month
