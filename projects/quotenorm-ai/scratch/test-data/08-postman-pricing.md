# Postman Pricing
Source: https://www.postman.com/pricing/
Captured: 2026-03-12
Format: URL (pricing page)

## Plans

### Free
- **Price:** $0
- **Users:** 1
- **API Calls:** 10,000/month
- **Integrations:** Up to 5
- **Collection Recovery:** 1 day
- **AI Credits:** 50/user/month
- **Monitoring:** 1,000 requests/month

### Solo
- **Price:** $9/month (billed annually)
- **Users:** 1
- **API Calls:** 100,000/month
- **Integrations:** Up to 10
- **Collection Recovery:** 30 days
- **AI Credits:** 400/user/month ($0.05/credit overage)
- **Monitoring:** 10,000 requests/month
- **Custom-branded docs**

### Team
- **Price:** $19/user/month (billed annually)
- **Users:** Unlimited
- **API Calls:** 1 million/month
- **Integrations:** Up to 50
- **Collection Recovery:** 90 days
- **AI Credits:** 400/user/month ($0.04/credit overage)
- **Monitoring:** 10,000 requests/month
- **Additional:** Team collaboration, SDK generation, workspace viewers, basic RBAC

### Enterprise
- **Price:** $49/user/month (billed annually)
- **Users:** Unlimited
- **API Calls:** 10 million/month
- **Integrations:** Up to 100
- **Collection Recovery:** 90 days
- **AI Credits:** 800/user/month pooled ($0.035/credit overage)
- **Monitoring:** 10,000 requests/month
- **Additional:** Private API Network, API Catalog, advanced RBAC, SCIM, audit logs, EU data residency, 24x5 support (24hr SLA)

## Security
- SSO/SAML: Team (add-on), Enterprise (included)
- Advanced RBAC: Enterprise only
- Audit logs: Enterprise only
- EU data residency: Enterprise only

## Support
- Free/Solo: Community & Email
- Team: Community & Email + Premium add-on available
- Enterprise: 24x5 (24hr SLA) + Premium add-on available
