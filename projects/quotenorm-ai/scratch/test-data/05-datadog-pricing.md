## Infrastructure Monitoring

### Free
- **Price:** $0
- **Features:** Core collection and visualization, 1-day metric retention, up to 5 hosts, 1,000+ integrations

### Pro
- **Price:** $15/host/month (annual); $18/host/month (on-demand)
- **Features:** 15-month metric retention, 100 custom metrics/host, 500 custom events/host, 5 containers/host, unlimited alerts

### Enterprise
- **Price:** $23/host/month (annual); $27/host/month (on-demand)
- **Features:** All Pro + machine learning alerts, Live Processes, Governance Console, 200 custom metrics/host, 1,000 custom events/host, 10 containers/host

### DevSecOps Pro
- **Price:** $22/host/month (annual); $27/host/month (on-demand)
- **Features:** All Infra Pro + Cloud Security Management Pro (CSPM, KSPM, vulnerability management, CIEM)

### DevSecOps Enterprise
- **Price:** $34/host/month (annual); $41/host/month (on-demand)
- **Features:** All Infra Enterprise + CSM Enterprise, file integrity monitoring, Workload Protection

## APM

### Standard
- **Price:** $31/host/month with Infrastructure (annual); $36/host/month standalone
- **Includes:** 150 GB span ingestion/host/month; 1M indexed spans/host/month

### Pro
- **Price:** $35/host/month with Infrastructure (annual); $41/host/month standalone
- **Includes:** All APM + Data Streams Monitoring

### Enterprise
- **Price:** $40/host/month with Infrastructure (annual); $47/host/month standalone
- **Includes:** All APM Pro + Continuous Profiler

## Log Management
- **Ingestion:** $0.10/GB/month
- **Standard Indexing:** $1.70/million events/month (annual); 3-30 day retention
- **Flex Storage:** $0.05/million events/month (annual); 1-15 months retention
- **Flex Logs Starter:** $0.60/million events/month (annual)
- **Log Forwarding:** $0.25/GB outbound/destination/month

## Other Products
- **Code Coverage:** $8/committer/month (annual)
- **Feature Flags:** Free <1M requests; $55/month for 1M-10M (annual)
- **Bits AI SRE:** $500/month per 20 investigations (annual)
- **Custom Metrics Add-on:** $1 per 100 custom metrics/month

## Terms
- Annual and on-demand billing available
- Multi-year/volume discounts for 500+ hosts
- SOC 2, PCI, HIPAA compliance supported
- SAML SSO, SCIM provisioning (Enterprise)
