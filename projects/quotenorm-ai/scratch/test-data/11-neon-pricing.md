## Plans

- **Price:** $0/month
- **Compute:** 100 CU-hours/month per project
- **Storage:** 0.5 GB per project
- **Max Compute:** 2 CU (8 GB RAM)
- **Projects:** 100
- **Branches:** 10/project
- **Auth:** Up to 60,000 MAUs
- **Time Travel:** 6-hour window
- **Support:** Community (Discord)

## All Plans Include
- Multi-AZ storage, read replicas, autoscaling, scale to zero
- Connection pooling (pgBouncer, up to 10,000)
- PostgreSQL extensions, built-in auth
- GDPR and ISO compliance
