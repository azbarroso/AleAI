## Personal Plans

### Individual
- **Annual:** $2.99/month (paid annually)
- **Monthly:** $3.99/month
- **Users:** Single user
- **Features:** Password generation, autosave/autofill, secure sharing, multi-device, breach alerts
- **Trial:** 14 days free

### Families
- **Annual:** $4.49/month (paid annually)
- **Monthly:** $5.99/month
- **Users:** Up to 6 (1 primary + 5 family)
- **Features:** All Individual + unlimited vault sharing, admin controls
- **Trial:** 14 days free

## Business Plans

### Teams Starter Pack
- **Price:** $19.95 flat/month (up to 10 users)
- **Features:** Password/data sharing, security alerts, role-based permissions, developer tools
- **Trial:** 14 days free

### Business
- **Annual:** $7.99/user/month
- **Monthly:** $9.99/user/month
- **Features:** All Teams + SSO (Okta, Entra ID, OneLogin, Duo), vault permissions, custom policies, Watchtower alerts
- **Support:** Mon-Fri 9AM-5PM EST, personalized training for 101+ users, dedicated account managers
- **Trial:** 14 days free
