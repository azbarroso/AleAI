## 5. Contract Terms

- **Term:** 3 years (36 months) from execution date
- **Billing:** Quarterly in advance (platform + users); monthly in arrears (infrastructure usage)
- **Payment terms:** Net 60
- **Auto-renewal:** 12-month renewal periods unless either party provides 90 days written notice
- **Price escalation:** Platform and user fees locked for 3 years; infrastructure subject to annual review with ≤7% cap
- **Termination for convenience:** After month 18, with 6 months notice and payment of 25% of remaining contract value
- **Termination for cause:** 30 days to cure after written notice of material breach
- **IP ownership:** Customer owns all data, models trained on customer data, and custom dashboards. DataStream retains platform IP and generic model architectures.
- **Data portability:** Full data export in standard formats (Parquet, CSV, JSON) within 30 days of contract end, at no additional charge
- **Indemnification:** DataStream indemnifies customer for IP infringement claims
- **Liability cap:** Greater of (a) $2M or (b) fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim
- **Governing law:** State of Delaware
