## Plans & Pricing

### Free Plan
- **Price:** $0/member/month
- **Target:** Individuals organizing personal projects
- **File Uploads:** Up to 5MB each
- **Page History:** 7 days
- **External Guests:** 10 limit
- **Charts:** 1

### Plus Plan
- **Monthly:** $10/member/month
- **Annual:** $120/member/year (saves 20%)
- **Target:** Small teams and professionals
- **File Uploads:** Unlimited (~5GB max per file)
- **Page History:** 30 days
- **External Guests:** Unlimited
- **Charts:** Unlimited

### Business Plan
- **Monthly:** $20/member/month
- **Annual:** $240/member/year
- **Target:** Growing businesses (Recommended)
- **Page History:** 90 days
- **Additional Features:** SAML SSO, Private teamspaces, Permission groups, Granular database permissions, Advanced teamspace security, Workspace analytics

### Enterprise Plan
- **Price:** Custom pricing
- **Page History:** Unlimited
- **Additional Features:** SCIM provisioning, Audit log, Admin content search, Domain management, Workspace consolidation, Managed users, Security & Compliance integrations (DLP, SIEM), Custom data retention
- **AI Data Retention:** Zero data retention with LLM providers
- **Support:** Premium support + dedicated Customer Success Manager

## AI Features
- **Notion AI Add-On:** $10/member/month (monthly) or $96/member/year
- **AI Credit Packs:** $100/month or $1,200/year
- All paid plans include AI core: chat, document generation, database autofill, translation
- Business+: Agent, Enterprise Search, AI Meeting Notes, Research Mode

## Add-Ons
- **Custom Domains:** $8/month annual or $10/month

## Billing Terms
- Per-seat model (charged per workspace member; guests free)
- Annual saves up to 20%
- Prorated charges for mid-month changes
- Refund: Full refund within 3 days (monthly) or 30 days (annual)
- EU/UK: Full refund within 14 days

## Student Plan
- Plus Plan features with 1-member limit, free with eligible school email
