# What is ContextSignal?

ContextSignal.ai helps project managers integrate AI into their actual work. Not in theory — in practice, with specific workflows, prompt templates, and tool recommendations built for how PMs operate.

The site is organised around real PM workflows — sprint planning, status reports, retrospectives, stakeholder updates, risk tracking — with ready-to-use prompts and guides for each one.

## What You'll Find Here

**Workflow guides** — step-by-step breakdowns showing how AI fits into specific PM tasks like sprint planning, retrospective facilitation, or stakeholder communication. These are the core of the site.

**Prompt template packs** — ready-to-use prompts organised by PM workflow, so you're not starting from a blank page. Our free pack includes 50 templates across 7 categories.

**Tool recommendations in context** — we cover AI tools like Notion, Monday.com, ClickUp, and Slack, but always through the lens of PM workflows. A tool isn't "good" or "bad" in the abstract — it's good or bad for the way you work.

**The Context Signal newsletter** — a biweekly email with one actionable AI workflow, one tool worth knowing about, and one prompt template you can copy straight into your tools.

## How We Work

ContextSignal uses AI in its editorial workflow, including drafting content. Our editorial standards, quality checks, and final decisions are human-defined and human-reviewed.

Every workflow guide and tool recommendation goes through a defined editorial process before it reaches you. We believe being transparent about this is more useful than pretending every word is hand-typed.
