# ContextSignal.ai

## Goal

Build a sustainable AI-powered digital product business targeting professionals, with content as the sales funnel. Target: $1k–$2k/month revenue by Month 12. Time budget: 2–3 hrs/week.

## Context

**What it is:** A product-first content site organised by audience vertical. Each vertical targets a professional role with workflow guides, prompt libraries, and digital products (prompt packs, templates, toolkits).

**Part of:** Signal Media Portfolio. Domain: contextsignal.ai

**Key constraint:** Content is Claude-generated with light human review. No direct domain expertise — content is research-based. This rules out SEO-first strategies that depend on E-E-A-T signals, original screenshots, or personal workflows.

**Business model (product-first):**

| Revenue stream | Weight |
|----------------|--------|
| Digital products (prompt packs, templates, toolkits) | **45%** |
| Affiliate commissions | 20% |
| Newsletter sponsorships | 20% |
| Display advertising | 5% |
| Community/consulting | 10% |

**Why products first:** Digital products sell on utility — buyers care if the prompts work, not whether the creator personally uses the tool daily. This is where Claude-generated content is genuinely strong.

**Content model — 2 tiers:**
- **Standard (1/week):** Claude-generated, quick scan. Workflow guides targeting long-tail keywords. Funnel to lead magnet.
- **Flagship (1/month):** Claude-generated, more thorough review. Comprehensive vertical-anchoring guides. Funnel to digital product.

**Total output:** ~4–5 posts/month.

**Verticals — focused launch:**
- **Start:** Project Managers only
- **Expand:** Sales Professionals only after PM is generating revenue

**Distribution strategy:** Newsletter-first (Beehiiv), community engagement (Reddit, LinkedIn). SEO as bonus, not dependency.

**Tech stack:** WordPress on Kinsta (~$35/mo), GeneratePress theme, Rank Math SEO, Claude API, Beehiiv email, Gumroad/LemonSqueezy for products, Pretty Links for affiliates, GA4 + Search Console.

**Monthly cost:** ~$50–70 early stage.

**Time commitment:** 2–3 hrs/week consistently.

**Key strategy doc:** `ContextSignal-Strategy-v6.md` in this folder — full strategy with revenue model, content plan, distribution, stop rules, and expansion playbook. Previous version (`ContextSignal-Strategy-v5.2.2-Final.md`) preserved for reference.

## Decisions

| Date | Decision | Rationale |

| 2026-03 | Pivot from tool reviews to audience-vertical model | Generic tool listicles too saturated; role-specific workflows differentiate and unlock digital products |
